Sometimes I need to learn a framework in *both present in its legacy
and modern* form in different project. I like to to take advantage of 
an available course, but **break** most of its functionality,
by **updating to all the newest package versions**.

Making it work again, teaches me both 'old and new ways', and gives a unique
perspective into directions a framework or tool chose to persue and improve.

Here is [an example](https://github.com/lynochka/Bootcamp/blob/main/redux/Readme.md)
 of I acquired skills in Redux with highlights of needed fixes inside.
